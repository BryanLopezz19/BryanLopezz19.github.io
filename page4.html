In my maps project I chose to do soccer stadiums. I chose these stadiums because I believe that visiting one of these stadiums is a very special thing that we all should enjoy. This website will help you locate soccer stadiums in the U.S. To locate one of the stadiums you just click on an icon and once you've click on it an image of the stadium will pop up. I used Google Maps API.
